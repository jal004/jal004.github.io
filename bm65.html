The BM65

Budget 65%

Keyboard Overview

The BM65 is a budget 65% keyboard manufactured by KP Republic with a blocker between the bottom row and arrow keys. The blocker allows for users to easily find the arrow keys during use. The 65% layout does not contain the function row and is one of the smallest layouts that still feature the arrow keys.

  • In-Stock Price: $60
  • Product Page

The mounting style of the BM65 is known as a "top" mount, meaning that the plate is secured to only the top-half of the case.

Build Specification

  • Keycaps: GMK Botanical Clones
  • Switches: Cherry MX Silent Reds w/ Gazzew clear tops
  • Plate: Steel plate
  • Stabilizers: Holee-modded Durock V2 stabilizers
  • Foam(s): N/A

The case of the BM65 is completely plastic, making it light and portable for travel. The case also features a low front height of 14mm. The size and weight of this keyboard makes it suitable as a travel keyboard.

Personal Review

The BM65 is an affordable keyboard that does not feature many modern characteristics, but satisfies my usages. The extremely low profile of the keyboard eliminates my concerns for wrist strain. Additionally, the rugged plastic construction of the BM65 paired with the silent switches make this keyboard suitable for working in quiet environments, and on the go.
